The Project


As UW's Institute of Health Metrics and Evaluation continues to grow their current network of over 3,400 collaborators, a serious bottleneck issue has arisen. Collaborators periodically require updates to their organization information in the IHME system, such as affiliations, specialties, and interests, and the UW IHME simply does not have the time to process all these requests. To solve this situation, we will be building a portal that allows collaborators with the proper credentials to update their organization’s information and vastly reduce the information change requests the IHME staff has to process.
